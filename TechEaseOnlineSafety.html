Online Safety

Learn simple ways to stay safe while using the internet and social media.

🔑 Creating Strong Passwords

Use a combination of letter numbers, and symbols.Avoid using personal information like birthdays or names.

⚠️ Avoid Suspicious Links

Do not click unknown links from messages or emails. They may contain scams or harmful websites.

👤 Protect Your Information

Never share passwords, OTPs, or private details online, especially with strangers.

📱 Safe Social Media Usage

Be careful about what you post online and avoid talking to suspicious accounts.

🔄 Keep Apps Updated

Updating apps and phones helps improve security and protects your device.
