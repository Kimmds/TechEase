Computer Tips

Learn simple and useful computer basics to help you work faster and more comfortably.

⌨️ Basic Keyboard Shortcuts

Use Ctrl + C to copy, Ctrl + V to paste, and Ctrl + Z to undo actions quickly.

🖱️ Using the Mouse Properly

Left click to select, right click for options, and double click to open files or apps.

📁 Organizing Files

Keep your files in folders so you can easily find documents, pictures, and videos.

⚙️ Shutting Down Properly

Always shut down your computer using the Start menu to avoid system problems.

🛡️ Keeping Your Computer Safe

Avoid downloading unknown files and always use trusted websites.
